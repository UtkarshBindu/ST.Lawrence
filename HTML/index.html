About Us

St. Lawrence Marathi High School is one of the renowned and top ranking State Board schools in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. St. Lawrence High School offers a live learning experience for each student from class I to class X while moulding their path towards a bright future. The massive ,impressive building is equipped with airy classrooms, sports facilities, science & computer labs, etc. We have well trained & qualified faculty and an unbeatable administrative staff who work in tandem to give our students a prime education which inculcates an attitude of consistent learning in them. Every St. Lawrence student participates in a lot of co-curricular activities which nurture their creative approach towards discovering the best version of themselves.

About the Founders

St. Lawrence High School, Aurangabad was founded by Mr. Collins Albuquerque and Mrs. Flavia Albuquerque in the year 1988 under the patronage of the St. Xavier's Education Trust. They believed that every child had the potential to excel academically and wanted to create a school that would provide a nurturing environment where students could develop their talents and abilities.
The founders believe in and commit to enhance the value of education and empower the students with competence to take up challenges in any form. The constant support from the parents also plays an important role in giving the school the recognition it deserves; for which the school remains eternally grateful to them.

Infrastructure

Functional & Well Equipped Classrooms

The school subscribes to the idea of keeping the classrooms well equipped with all the essential learning materials. Well ventilated and bright classrooms encourage students to take an interest in education. Learning materials like display boards help students to exhibit their knowledge as well as creativity in their own space as they progress through the prescribed syllabus. All the classrooms at St. Lawrence High School are equipped with blackboards, furniture and skid-proof flooring.

Computer Room

The spacious computer room with numerous sets of computers enables the students to learn, develop and explore different applications and concepts of technology under the guidance of teachers. The computers are safe and centrally connected to the server. The computer lab not only offers integrated computer skills to the senior students but also provides exposure to the novices.

Science Laboratory

To support theoretical teaching in Science and to develop interest in scientific outlook, the school has well-equipped Science Laboratories. Every year the Science lab is equipped with the latest apparatus, instruments, models, specimens, charts, etc. according to the requirement. Lab is well designed to provide firsthand experience to the students on their own.

Playground

The school's spacious playground provides an ideal outdoor space for students to engage in physical activities and foster social interaction. The playground offers a fun and safe environment for children to explore, run, jump, climb and play games. The open area allows ample room for sports such as basketball, football, and volleyball, while the swings, slides, and monkey bars offer younger children a chance to develop their motor skills and coordination.

Co-Curricular Activities

At St. Lawrence High School, students also have the option to choose from a vast variety of activities to complement their interests and hone their individual talents. The activities can be broadly categorized into Sports, Fine Art , Performing Arts, Music, Etc

Sports

All the physical activities are conducted in the school. The students are supervised and guided by trained sports coaches. The school has outdoor and indoor sports with all types of sporting equipment made available to students. The school's ever-expanding repertoire currently includes Badminton, Basketball, Cricket, Chess, etc. We encourage students to actively participate in sports for their wellbeing and health.

Arts

The students of St. Lawrence High School are trained by qualified art teachers. They are instructed with the basics of sketching, drawing, collage making, colouring, painting, model making, and many other art techniques. Exposure to art allows our students to express themselves.
